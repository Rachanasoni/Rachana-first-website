Mr. R. C. Soni is a dedicated principal of Govt. HighSchool affiliated by Board of Highschool & Intermediate Education Allahabad. He started his teaching journey in year 1977 from almorah.He has been a great writer, poet,critic, speaker & motivator.

In his 40 year career he has been a great source of energy, motivation, dedication to all his students and fellow teachers. As the father of four children, He has a perspective that every children is special and has its own quality and talent. He also beleives that We need to focus on learning.

He is passionately interested in civil society and how that can be grown through relationships, trust, education and technology. He beleives reading as central to learning and culture. This is a deeply held belief. Students needs opportunities to participate in discussion about the issues of the day and learn about democratic processes.

Helping teachers and educational leaders develop Personal Learning Networks (PLNs) has been an area of interest for a decade. He strongly support the idea that If educators are able to use blended learning concepts to facilitate their own professional development it is more likely students will benefit.

During these years He wrote many journals, lesson plans, and presented his ideas in many conferences for principals, deputy principals, Hindi teachers, counsellors, technology enthusiasts and administrators on practical ways to collaborate, co-operate and use digital technologies to connect. His focus at school is on teaching & learning using technology.

Whenever it comes to enlightening and motivating people on the moral path of progress, he leaves no stones unturned. It is his spirit towards humanity that keeps him going. His achievements, speeches and his entire life is an institution of motivation itself.

Mr. R. C. Soni has not only earned respect, love and admiration, he has showed the path to millions as well. He rose as a silver lining and the fresh air of spring that took the masses by surprise and pleasure at the same time. He has set records as a poet, performer and motivator and the saga still continues as he continues to influence lives globally.
